Meltdown

Humanity's solar-radiation plant on Venus is close to a catastrophic meltdown. The sun's rays, which once powered the colony, are now deadly. The player must counteract these radioactive gamma rays for as long as possible to give the military time to evacuate civilians from the planet.

Meltdown is a whimsical game written in Python. It utilizes Python's GUI module, Tkinter, along with threading and color comparison functionality.
